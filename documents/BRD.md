# Business Requirement Document (BRD)
**Project Name:** EmPulse - Employee Recognition & Reward Platform
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-02-02
**Author:** EmPulse Tech Team

## 1. Executive Summary
EmPulse is a peer-to-peer (P2P) recognition platform designed to foster a culture of appreciation within EmbedIT. It allows employees to recognize colleagues, earn points, and redeem real-world rewards, addressing the need for a modern, engaging, and global application.

## 2. Project Objectives
-   **Increase Engagement:** Boost employee morale through frequent, visible recognition.
-   **Global Connection:** Bridge the gap between distributed teams (Vietnam, Czech Republic, etc.).
-   **Tangible Rewards:** Provide a transparent system to convert social recognition into physical/digital value.
-   **Cultural Alignment:** Reinforce company values (Collaboration, Innovation) through badging.

## 3. Stakeholders
-   **Usage:** All Employees (vote senders/receivers).
-   **Management:** Managers (approvers, budget holders).
-   **Administration:** HR Team (catalog management, user management, reporting).
-   **Tech:** IT/Dev Team (maintenance, integration).

## 4. Scope

### 4.1 In-Scope
-   **User Auth:** Login/Logout, Role-based access (Employee, Admin).
-   **Voting System:** P2P voting with messages and point allocation.
-   **Wallets:** Quota Wallet (monthly limit for sending) and Reward Wallet (accumulated points).
-   **Leaderboard:** Monthly and All-time top contributors.
-   **Redemption Store:** Catalog of physical/digital items, order processing.
-   **Admin Panel:** Manage users, rewards, and view vote logs.
-   **Landing Page:** Public facing introduction.

### 4.2 Out-of-Scope (Phase 1)
-   Mobile Native App (Responsive Web App only).
-   Integration with Payroll systems (Standalone for now).
-   Complex HRIS synchronization (Manual import/export).

## 5. Success Metrics
-   **Adoption Rate:** >80% of employees active monthly.
-   **Velocity:** >500 votes set per month.
-   **Redemption:** >30% of points redeemed quarterly.
